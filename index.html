About Blank

We are ever growing group of problem solvers, expert digital solution creators (developers, product managers, data scientists, UX designers etc.) and social workers who have come forth to 

  • Identify Blanks
  • Ideate Solutions
  • Fill the 'Blanks'
Our Team Join Us
1

Identify Problems

We looks for different social or mass scale individual problems (or blanks) around us. Survey about the problem to identify its impact and severity..
2

Ideate Solutions

Conduct multiple ideation sessions amongst volunteers in presence of field mentors and experts to think through the solutions of these problems.
3

Fill the 'Blank'

Form a team of product managers, experienced developers, to plan, implement and market the solutions.
